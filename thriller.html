Thriller - Michael Jackson

About the Album

"Thriller" by Michael Jackson is the best-selling album of all time, redefining the music industry and setting an unparalleled standard for pop music. Released in 1982, this masterpiece blends pop, rock, and funk, breaking genre barriers and captivating audiences worldwide. With iconic tracks like "Billie Jean" and "Beat It," Jackson cemented his legacy as the King of Pop, forever shaping the music and entertainment world.
